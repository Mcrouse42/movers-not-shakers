How To Use Our Site

Enter any City, State or Country in the search bar. The weather for that location will appear directly below. If the area has had any seismic activity recently a red circle will be displayed. The size of the circle relates to the magnitude of the most recent earthquake. The larger the circle - the bigger the earthquake in that location.
